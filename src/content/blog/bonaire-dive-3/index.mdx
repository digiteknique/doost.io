Third day on the same reef and still finding new sections of it - Bellafonte's house reef is bigger than it looks from the dock. Found a good ledge partway through the dive with a blue parrotfish working over the coral.

<Photo src={parrotfish} alt="Reef with a blue parrotfish grazing among the coral" caption="Blue parrotfish grazing along the reef" />

## Marine life

The highlight was a dense school of blue/black chromis balled up over one section of reef - dozens of them, packed tight enough to look like a single moving shape.
