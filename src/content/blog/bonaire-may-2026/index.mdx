[Trip summary — who went, where we stayed, why Bonaire.]

Bonaire's whole thing is shore diving — rent a truck, grab tanks, drive to a site, and walk in whenever you want. Four of the five dives were right off the dock at Bellafonte, our house reef for the week. The fifth was Salt Pier, a working pier you can only dive when there's no ship in — worth timing the trip around.

## The dives

1. [Dive 1: Bellafonte House Reef](/blog/bonaire-dive-1/) — first dive, shaking off the rust
2. [Dive 2: Bellafonte House Reef](/blog/bonaire-dive-2/) — coral heads, rope sponges, a proper reef wall
3. [Dive 3: Bellafonte House Reef](/blog/bonaire-dive-3/) — a dense chromis school, best fish encounter yet
4. [Dive 4: Salt Pier](/blog/bonaire-dive-4/) — the pilings, sunrays, the one dive off-site
5. [Dive 5: Bellafonte House Reef](/blog/bonaire-dive-5/) — closed it out with a sea turtle
