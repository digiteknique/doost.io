Whale sharks had been on my bucket list for years - I just never got around to actually planning a trip around them. Christina did. She put this together for me, and it's one of the best gifts I've ever gotten.

We stayed at a beachfront place along the coast and were out early the next morning, in Oslob, Cebu - one of the few places in the world where whale sharks show up reliably close to shore. It was a shallow scuba dive right off an outrigger boat, sharks cruising a few feet under everyone in the water, snorkelers and divers mixed in together.

<Photo src={approach} alt="A whale shark approaching a group in the water near the boat's outrigger" caption="First one showed up within a minute or two of getting in the water" />

Nothing really prepares you for the scale of them up close. They're enormous - the biggest fish in the ocean - but completely unbothered by a bunch of people in the water above them. Slow, deliberate, gliding just under the surface.

<Photo src={closePass} alt="A whale shark passing close beneath a group at the surface" caption="They pass a lot closer than you'd expect" />

<Photo src={diverPortrait} alt="Diver giving an OK sign at the surface with a school of fish overhead" caption="Between passes - the fish traffic up top was its own show" />

The best moments were the backlit ones - camera pointed up at a shark's silhouette with the sun breaking through the surface behind it. Hard to overstate how good the visibility was.

<Photo src={silhouette} alt="A whale shark silhouetted against the sun-dappled surface, a diver visible in the distance below" caption="One of the calmer passes, sunlight cutting through the surface" />

And then there was this one - a shark surfaced close enough that I could reach out and nearly touch it on the way past.

<Photo src={closeEncounter} alt="Close-up selfie with a whale shark's spotted head and mouth right behind, giving a thumbs up" caption="About as close as it gets" />

## What I'd do differently

Nothing, honestly. If I went back I'd just try to stay in the water longer - the encounter windows go by fast.

## The bucket list item

Been wanting to do this since I first learned whale sharks existed. Christina turned "someday" into an actual itinerary, and I got to spend a morning in the water with the biggest fish on the planet because of it.
