## Notes

Longer dive, more time to actually look around instead of just checking gear. The reef here builds up in these big mounded coral heads with sand channels between them - easy to just follow a channel and see what's along it.

## Marine life

Small reef fish throughout, nothing rare - mostly chromis and wrasses moving through the coral. Good light for most of the dive, made for easier photos than usual.
